WIE HACK 2.0

All Women 36hr Online Hackathon

General Instructions

  • It is a 36-hour online hackathon.
  • There will be a total of 6 rounds and submission at every stage is compulsory to proceed in the competition
  • Mentoring Sessions and workshops are compulsory for the participants to attend as they describe what is to be done in a particular round.
  • There will be a live chat option, where the participants may ask their queries or doubts.
  • In case you face difficulty during submission or while watching the Livestream, contact the technical team immediately.
  • You must have a stable internet connection during the entire length of the hackathon to ensure uninterrupted streaming
  • Participants are expected to maintain the code of conduct.
  • At any time if the participants are found disobeying any of the rules of the competition, they will be disqualified from the hackathon.
